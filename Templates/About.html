About Me

My goal with this project is to provide a revolutionary solution for the deaf community by offering hand typing capabilities akin to voice typing. Recognizing the significance of accessibility and inclusivity, I aim to bridge communication barriers and empower individuals with alternative methods of expression. Hand typing serves as a vital tool for deaf individuals, granting them independence and facilitating seamless interaction in both personal and professional settings. This project leverages cutting-edge technologies such as machine learning and computer vision, with ml5.js and p5.js playing pivotal roles in image classification and real-time video processing. By embracing these tools, I strive to create an intuitive and effective platform that revolutionizes accessibility for the deaf community. Signed with dedication and passion,

Brody Abowitz
